Payment Status In Purchase Order
Summary
This module enhances the Purchase Order functionality in Odoo by adding payment status tracking and amount paid visibility, including:

1.Display of payment status (abk_payment_status) on Purchase Orders
2.Tracking of the amount paid (abk_amount_paidd) per Purchase Order
3.Visual indicators (badges) for payment statuses (Not Paid, Fully Paid, Partially Paid, Nothing to Pay)
4.Filtering and grouping Purchase Orders by payment status in search views
5.Compatible with Odoo 18.0


Features
✅ Track and display payment status directly on Purchase Order forms and tree views
✅ Show the amount paid for each Purchase Order with currency support
✅ Visual badges with color-coded statuses (red for Not Paid, green for Fully Paid, yellow for Partially Paid, blue for Nothing to Pay)
✅ Group and filter Purchase Orders by payment status in search
✅ Restricted visibility of payment status to authorized users via security groups
✅ Compatible with Odoo 18.0
