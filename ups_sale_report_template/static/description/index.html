How It Works

Three simple steps to stunning sale reports

STEP 1

Configure Your Report Settings

Navigate to Settings → Custom Report to enable custom reports for your company. Select your preferred sale template, customize primary, secondary & header colors, and upload watermark or background images — all from one intuitive panel.

Enable / Disable per company
Choose from 10 templates
Color picker for branding
STEP 2

Select Custom Paper Format

Go to Settings → Technical → Reports and assign the A4 - Sale Custom Size paper format to the Quotation / Order report. This ensures your sale documents print with pixel-perfect margins and spacing.

Dedicated sale paper format
Precise margin control
Separate from invoice formats
STEP 3

Verify Report Action

Under Settings → Technical → Reports, locate the Quotation / Order report action. Confirm the correct template name and model are assigned so the sale report prints flawlessly.
